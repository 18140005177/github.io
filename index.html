赛事简介Tournament Introduction

Superkit青少年高尔夫精英联赛年度总决赛是一项面向全国优秀青少年高尔夫选手的顶级赛事，旨在为青少年选手提供一个展示才华、交流学习的平台，推动中国青少年高尔夫运动的发展。
The Superkit Junior Golf Elite League Annual Finals is a top-level event for outstanding junior golfers from across the country, aiming to provide a platform for young players to showcase their talents, exchange learning, and promote the development of junior golf in China.

赛事地点Location

东莞观澜湖高尔夫球会Mission Hills Golf Club Dongguan

东莞观澜湖高尔夫球会是世界第一大高尔夫球会，拥有多个国际标准的高尔夫球场，是举办顶级高尔夫赛事的理想场地。球会设施完善，服务一流，为参赛选手提供最佳的比赛环境。
Mission Hills Golf Club Dongguan is the world's largest golf club, featuring multiple international standard golf courses, making it the ideal venue for hosting top-tier golf tournaments. The club has complete facilities and first-class services, providing the best competition environment for participating players.

地址：广东省东莞市塘厦镇观澜湖大道1号Address: No. 1, Mission Hills Avenue, Tangxia Town, Dongguan City, Guangdong Province

交通：距离深圳宝安国际机场约45分钟车程，距离东莞市中心约30分钟车程Transportation: Approximately 45 minutes by car from Shenzhen Bao'an International Airport, approximately 30 minutes by car from Dongguan City Center
